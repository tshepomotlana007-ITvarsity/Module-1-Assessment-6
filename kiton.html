Product Description

Two-Piece Double-Breasted Suit
Fashioned in Naples by no fewer than 25 different people, this double-breasted two-piece suit by Kiton has been hand-cut and tacked with an obsessive attention to detail. Staying true to the brand's founding philosophy of high-quality garments, this style boasts a soft cashmere-blend for all-day-long comfort.

Master artisans of cutting and stitching but, first and foremost, genuinely passionate. Expert hands and eyes which, throughout all the production stages, add value to the product, making each piece unique and inimitable. They sublimely link creativity, spirituality and artistic expression through the use of precious yarns and fabrics.

Our garment production is a process that requires experience and meticulousness but, above all, it necessitates research.

Indeed, the starting point for the construction of each tailored item, both in leather and fabric, is the painstaking analysis of every detail of the fabric. From the texture to the color and the pattern.
